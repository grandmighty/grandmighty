Key features of minimalist design in architecture include:<\/p>\n

    \n
  • Perfect field connections, meeting the walls and roof require a neat handling.<\/li>\n
  • The use of light – as an element that can provide dramatic effect. Games artificial or natural light to produce depth of field effects.<\/li>\n
  • Flat roof, or nearly flat for buildings in tropical climates.<\/li>\n
  • Open spaces and large windows, open spaces useful to compensate for the building, while the windows provide views out more freely.<\/li>\n
  • Open space, eliminating the wall material \u2013 for example the kitchen and dining room in one space.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n
