With little else left to do on the RX-7, I’m finally taking a step back and slowing down the pace. I fear if I continue to plummet headlong at my usual pace, I will end up overstepping and upgrading what doesn’t need to be touched. <\/p>\n

Now, in my times of leisure, I have assumed the role of a small-time parts collector\/hoarder. <\/p>\n

I gave away my original FEED shift knob<\/a> because its thread pattern would no longer work with the MGW Short Shifter<\/a>. I liked the shift knob so I decided to contact FEED directly and asked them to custom machine me a M12x1.75 version to fit my current setup. <\/p>\n

<\/a><\/p>\n

While browsing Yahoo Auctions Japan, I came across this red Mazdaspeed horn button and grabbed it. These particular red ones are becoming rare. <\/p>\n

<\/a><\/p>\n

Continuing the Mazdaspeed theme and scratching an itch I’ve had for a long time, I also picked up a Mazdaspeed titanium e-brake handle. I found this one on eBay from a Malaysian seller, of all places. <\/p>\n

<\/a><\/p>\n

I didn’t let the fact that I no longer own RegaMaster wheels<\/a> stop me from picking up a pair of black Marquis Promada center caps, which are far harder to come by than the silver\/white counterparts. <\/p>\n

<\/a><\/p>\n

I will throw on the Mazdaspeed horn button soon, but I’m vacillating on whether or not I should run the e-brake handle. <\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
