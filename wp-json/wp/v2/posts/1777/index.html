It seemed like the van was handling a bit strange, and having to hold the wheel cranked to the left didn’t seem right either. At first I thought something popped off on the front suspension. It was either that, or someone brutally rammed into the van over night, thus destroying the chassis (and I somehow skipped over the damages). Very good and profoundly reasonable guesses, no doubt, but I decided to be empirical and pulled over to physically look. Sure enough, it was just a flat. Not too bummed about it, these tires held up pretty well given their age and poor condition.<\/p>\n
