Finding humans that share the endearing qualities of a dog can be hard to do. It’s amazing to think that we are able to befriend these creatures and keep them as companions. Unlike humans, dogs are not corrupted by the concepts of egoism, cynicism, shallowness, pettiness, etc. Of course, just like people, you have your good and bad… There are dogs with poor temperament and there are the useless ones that maintain a low level function with no greater purpose other than to eat and poop. <\/p>\n

I can say I am lucky to have my dog Blue. I know he is loyal<\/em> and that’s one of the best things you can ask for in this day and age. <\/p>\n

People can be quick to discard their allegiances with you. People have side agendas and motives. People lack the understanding of loyalty.<\/p>\n
