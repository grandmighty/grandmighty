This photoshoot was actually done a couple weeks ago, it’s just taken me this long to finally post it. I met up with the owner of the car, Benny, near the Dumbarton Bridge and went over to the fishing pier for these shots. Clean car all around, and very low. Literally an inch off the ground, the front carbon fiber lip would scrape over normal fluctuations in the road. Those 19″ Volks were nicely fitted into the wells with that stance. I took a LOT of photos and did a lot of filtering. I’ve only gone through the first half, the next part will come later.<\/p>\n
