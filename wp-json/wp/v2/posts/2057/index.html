This son of a bitch was expensive, but a necessity. The Miata is incomplete without a lip in the front. I’m not a big fan of the Garage Vary lip, but the OEM R-Package lip is nice and subtle.<\/p>\n

I installed the lip in the evening sometime this week. What I thought would be a simple, quick job turned into a 4 hour ordeal. It wasn’t going to be as easy as drilling some holes and using zip ties for me. I spent a lot of time getting everything lined up and drilling out ALL the necessary holes and using bolts. The sides were a bit tricky to fit right. Eventually the lip was secured.<\/p>\n

This lip is probably going to be 50% of all the body mods I plan on doing. Stock body is key…<\/p>\n
