Namely, a tune is mandatory. When temperatures are cold or the engine is not fully warm, it has the tendency to stall out when idling. Otherwise, drivability is mostly fine except for some low-speed bucking once the car is moving.<\/p>\n

When the rain stopped yesterday, I met up with a local photographer named Kace to do a quick feature shoot. More on that later once his pictures are ready. While on location, I had my camera with me as well and grabbed a couple pictures of my own.<\/p>\n
