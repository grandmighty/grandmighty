When I was looking around for Miata exhausts, it didn’t take long before I realized that the available options are thin. So I went another route. I contacted ARTech Fabrication<\/a> from Texas and contracted a custom build.\u00a0I’m done with big fart cans, I wanted something more sophisticated. Since the choice was under my control, I went with a pie-cut dolphin tail\/turn-down style.<\/p>\n

The exhaust is made from 16 gauge Stainless Steel, 2.5″ piping to a Magnaflow Race muffler and out a 3″ tip. Fully tig-welded. At the end, I added in a test pipe with a flex joint. There is a turnaround time, but all good things are worth the wait.<\/p>\n
